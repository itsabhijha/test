About Me

Presently Working as an Assistant Professor, I. K. Gujral Punjab Technical University amritsar. Since November 2020

Dr. R. K. Kumawat B’91, in Sikar Rajasthan, received B. Tech degree in Electrical Engineering, in 2011; M. Tech in power system, and Ph.D. in Power Electronics and Electric Drive from Rajasthan Technical University in 2014 and 2020 respectively. His research interest includes power electronics, multilevel inverter, Z-source converter drives, control, power quality, and electrical machine. Dr. Kumawat authored two books with national and international publishers in Germany. He has published more than 30 research papers in journals and conferences. He has got the certificate of best paper award for various papers at international conferences. He has received honours from the governor of Rajasthan and various government and social bodies for social services. He is an active member of various professional bodies; reviewer of high impact factor international journals, and member of the Editorial Board in science publication group, USA. He has worked as a Senior Research Fellow for the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) project sponsored by the Government of India. He has teaching and various administrative skill at UG and PG levels. Currently, he is working as an assistant professor on contract basis at I K Gujral Punjab Technical University, Punjab.
